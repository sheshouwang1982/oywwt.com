About the Game

The Memory Game is designed to test and improve your memory skills in an engaging and fun way. Perfect for players of all ages, this game will help sharpen your mind while having a great time!

How to Play

The game consists of 20 levels. In each level, a sequence of numbers will appear on the screen for 5 seconds. Memorize the sequence and enter it correctly when the timer ends.

  • Level 1 starts with a 1-digit number.
  • Each level increases the number of digits by 1.
  • You have 5 seconds to memorize each sequence.
  • Submit your answer to proceed to the next level.
