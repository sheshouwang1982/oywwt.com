Discover What's New: Everyone Who Pays for Slack Can Now Try Its AI Tools

Alison Perry

Slack has expanded access to its AI-powered tools, making them available to all paid subscribers. This significant update brings powerful AI capabilities directly into the workplace communication platform, transforming how teams collaborate, manage information, and streamline their workflows.

This comprehensive guide explores the new AI features, their practical applications, and how they can enhance productivity and collaboration in modern workplaces.

Overview of Slack's AI Tools

Slack's AI integration represents a major step forward in workplace productivity, offering intelligent assistance directly within the communication platform that millions of users rely on daily.

Key AI Features:

  • Smart Summaries: Automatic conversation and channel summaries
  • AI Search: Intelligent search across messages and files
  • Content Generation: AI-assisted writing and responses
  • Workflow Automation: AI-powered workflow suggestions
  • Knowledge Management: Intelligent information organization

Smart Summaries Feature

Conversation Summaries

The AI can automatically generate summaries of long conversations:

  • Key Points: Extract main discussion points
  • Action Items: Identify tasks and decisions
  • Participants: Highlight key contributors
  • Timeline: Organize information chronologically

Channel Summaries

For busy channels, AI provides daily or weekly summaries:

  • Activity Overview: Summary of channel activity
  • Important Updates: Highlight critical information
  • Trend Analysis: Identify patterns and trends
  • Resource Links: Compile relevant resources

AI-Powered Search

Intelligent Search Capabilities

Slack's AI search goes beyond simple keyword matching:

  • Semantic Search: Understand context and meaning
  • Natural Language Queries: Search using conversational language
  • Cross-Platform Search: Search across messages, files, and apps
  • Smart Suggestions: Suggest relevant content automatically

Search Examples

-- Find discussions about project deadlines
"Show me conversations about project deadlines"

-- Search for specific decisions
"What was decided about the budget?"

-- Find resources shared last week
"Show me files shared last week about marketing"

Smart Workflow Suggestions
